On 2 November 2025, Nikita Obraztsov gave a talk at the online conference 'Buddha, Brain, and the Neurophysiology of Happiness', organised by the Tergar Meditation Centre. The talk was titled 'How Religions Answer the Question of Meaning: Finding Common Ground in Diversity'. Other participants included neuroscientist Richard Davidson (University of Wisconsin) and meditation master Mingyur Rinpoche.
